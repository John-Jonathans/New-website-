Meet Our Founder

Dr. Bright Otso

Dr. Bright Otso is a visionary leader, teacher, and pastor with a passion for spreading the Gospel and transforming lives. With over 20 years of ministry experience, he founded Light House Communion International with a mission to be a beacon of hope in Jos and beyond.

Dr. Otso holds a Doctorate in Divinity from the International Theological Seminary and has dedicated his life to serving God and community. His teachings focus on practical Christian living, faith development, and community empowerment.

Under his leadership, Light House Communion has grown into a vibrant community of believers committed to worship, discipleship, and outreach.
